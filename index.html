How to use GameStop NFT Wallet:

Use google chrome on a PC/Mac

Go to wallet.gamestop.com


Hit the download button and get the GameStop Wallet browser extension on chrome webstore. Click add to chrome.

Click the puzzle piece in the top right of a chrome window and click GameStop Wallet

Select the option to "Recover Wallet" and enter the 12 word private key to access your wallet
