How to use GameStop NFT Wallet:

Use google chrome on a PC/Mac

Go to wallet.gamestop.com


Hit the download button and you'll go to the GameStop Wallet browser extension on chrome webstore. Click add to chrome.

After adding the wallet to chrome, click the puzzle piece in the top right of a chrome window and click GameStop Wallet

Select the option to "Recover Wallet" and enter the 12 word private key to access your wallet. Set a password to access your wallet on your computer. This can function as a backup to losing your 12 word private key so long as the account is not erased from your browser, or you lose/break your computer. If you do lose your 12 word mnemonic, you should create a new wallet with a newly generated phrase immediately and send everything to its public address from the account you can't find the private key to.
